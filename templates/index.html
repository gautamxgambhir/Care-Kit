Frequently Asked Questions

What is Care Kit?

Care Kit is your trusted partner in mental and physical well-being, offering resources for anxiety, depression, and self-care. We provide expert advice, coping techniques, and tools to help you lead a healthier and more balanced life.

What is Care Bot?

Care Bot is a supportive, friendly chatbot designed to provide emotional support and helpful guidance for mental and physical health issues, offering comfort and solutions for problems like anxiety and depression.

Is my conversation with Care Bot private?

Yes, all conversations with Care Bot are confidential, ensuring your privacy and well-being are respected.

How can I access the resources?

All our resources are available online through our website. You can browse articles, tips, and tools on various topics related to mental health, fitness, and overall well-being.

Is Care Kit free to use?

Yes! Care Kit offers free access to all its resources and content. We believe in making mental health support accessible to everyone, anytime, anywhere.

Can I contact a professional for advice?

While Care Kit provides helpful resources, we recommend consulting a certified healthcare professional for personalized advice. You can find helplines and expert recommendations on our platform.

How do I get updates about new resources?

You can stay updated by signing up for our newsletter. You’ll receive the latest tips, articles, and information directly to your inbox.
